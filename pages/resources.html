Evaluation Rubrics

Final Pitch (Judges) and TikTok Reflection (Trainers) — two separate 100% scores.

⌄

TikTok Reflection Challenge Rubric

50 raw marks × 2 = 100%
  • Each team submits daily TikTok-style reflection videos for Day 1, Day 2 and Day 3
  • Assessed separately from Final Pitch 100%
  • Trainers evaluate 10 scoring criteria worth 5 marks each
Daily Design Thinking Reflection — Day 1, Day 2 and Day 3 reflections15 marks
Learning, Insight & Evidence — learning, insight and template proof15 marks
Teamwork, Communication & Clarity — team roles and clear explanation10 marks
Creativity, Ethics & TikTok Presentation — creative, respectful and suitable video10 marks
Total raw marks50 × 2 = 100%

Final Pitch Rubric

50 raw marks × 2 = 100%
  • Evaluated separately as 100% by Judges, using the T14 Final Pitching Template
  • Duration: 8–10 min presentation + 3 min Q&A
  • Assessed separately from TikTok 100%
  • 10 scoring criteria × 5 marks = 50 raw marks; Pitching Score = Raw Score × 2 = /100
Presentation & Communication — 2 criteria × 5 marks10 marks
Content & Innovative Thinking — 5 DT phase criteria × 5 marks25 marks
Feasibility, Heritage & SDG Impact — 1 criterion × 5 marks5 marks
Team Collaboration & Q&A Handling — balanced participation with evidence-based answers5 marks
Language Use — clear, appropriate and professional English5 marks
