🎥 Daily Team Challenge

TikTok Reflection Format

Each team creates one short reflection video for Day 1, Day 2 and Day 3. The video should clearly show the team’s Design Thinking progress, key learning and completed evidence for the day.

100%TikTok Reflection Challenge
Duration
1–3 minutes per video
Video Size
Vertical TikTok format · 9:16
Language
English or bilingual English–Malay
Style
Creative, clear, respectful and educational
Submission
Upload the TikTok video link through the official submission platform
Daily Output
Day 1 + Day 2 + Day 3 reflection videos
#CAMP21#SmartDT#HeritageInnovation#DesignThinking#PoliteknikMalaysia

Suggested Video Structure

  1. 1
    Introduce team name and daily focus.
  2. 2
    Show short clips of team discussion, activity or prototype work.
  3. 3
    Explain the main insight, idea or learning of the day.
  4. 4
    Show evidence of completed templates or prototype progress.
  5. 5
    End with one reflection: “Today, our team learned that…”

Recording Reminder

Please record respectfully. Get permission before recording faces, interviews, artisans or public activities. Avoid inappropriate music, captions or content.
