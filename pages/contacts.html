Help & Support

Contact the secretariat, join the Telegram community, or access the Smart DT App for any camp-related queries.

Frequently Asked Questions

⏰ When is the hard deadline for pitch deck submission? ＋
All final pitch presentations must be completed and trainer-reviewed by Day 3 evening. Pitch duration is 8–10 minutes + 3 minutes Q&A (2 questions max).
⚖️ How are tie-breakers resolved? ＋
Ties are resolved by comparing the isolated Content & Innovative Thinking score (45%) first, followed by the Feasibility & Impact score (20%). Chief Judge Dr. Siti Zubaidah binti Ibrahim holds final authority on all judging decisions.
🔄 Do accompanying lecturers moderate their own polytechnic's team? ＋
No. Accompanying lecturers operate as unbiased, rotating facilitators across all 10 teams. They are strictly barred from remaining stationed with groups from their home institution.
📱 How do I submit templates on the Smart DT App? ＋
Open the Smart DT App, select your team, navigate to the current phase, and fill in each template (T01–T14). Templates auto-save and sync to the central dashboard. Your trainer can view your progress in real-time.
🎥 What is the TikTok Reflection Challenge? ＋
Each team creates one 2–3 minute TikTok-style vertical video reflecting on their entire DT journey — covering Empathise, Define, Ideate, Prototype and Test/Reflect phases. This is evaluated separately by trainers (/15 raw → /100). It's about genuine reflection, not perfect editing.
📋 Does T10 Prototype Direction Plan need trainer sign-off? ＋
Yes. T10 must be reviewed and signed off by your assigned phase trainer before any prototype build work begins. This is a mandatory checkpoint.
👔 What is the attire guideline? ＋
Workshop days: Smart-casual. Closing Ceremony (Thursday): Batik. Don't forget to bring outerwear for the Hop-On Hop-Off night activity on Day 1.
🎒 What should I bring? ＋
Laptop/Tablet (required for workshops), stationery, extension socket (to share power), mobile phone with sufficient mobile data, and your medicine if needed. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are provided for participants.
