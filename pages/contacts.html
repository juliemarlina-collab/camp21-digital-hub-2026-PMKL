Frequently Asked Queries

⏰ When is the hard deadline for slide submission? ＋
All final presentation decks must be fully submitted via the app portal terminal by Day 3 at 9:00 PM strictly. Late submissions automatically trigger a lock state.
⚖️ How do tie-breaker sequences resolve overall ties? ＋
Ties collapse strictly following the isolated evaluation value of Cultural Heritage Relevance first, followed by the isolated Prototype Quality score. Chief Judge Dr. Siti Zubaidah renders ultimate decisions.
🔄 Do accompanying lecturers moderate their own polytechnics? ＋
No. Accompanying lecturers operate as unbiased, rotating facilitators across all teams and are strictly barred from staying with groups originating from their home institutions.
