Judge Checklist Before Scoring

Use this as a quick conduct guide while watching the pitch presentation.

Before Pitch

Review Evidence

Check team submissions, daily reflection status and prototype evidence if available.

During Pitch

Observe Fairly

Focus on the team’s explanation, confidence, clarity and connection to the DT process.

After Q&A

Score Individually

Record marks and comments before discussing with other judges to reduce bias.

Important Judge Guidelines

Use submissions as evidence, not as the full score.
Final score should reflect both submitted work and live pitch performance.
Do not request new work on Day 4.
Teams should present what has already been developed, tested and prepared.
Keep rubrics and internal notes confidential.
Only official results should be announced by the secretariat.
Ask neutral questions.
Questions should clarify process, impact, prototype decisions and user feedback.
Look for improvement evidence.
T12–T13 should show how user feedback shaped the final prototype or pitch.
