Your Role at CAMP21

Three core duties across all four days.

Monitor Team Progress

Walk between team clusters during active work sessions. Note which phase each group is currently executing and flag any groups that appear stuck or off-track.

Guide Checkpoint Sign-offs

At each phase transition (Empathise → Define → Ideate → Prototype → Test), confirm team outputs meet the minimum template requirements before advancing.

Support Logistics

Assist with material distribution, room transitions, printing requests, and any venue-related needs. Coordinate with the secretariat table for urgent matters.
