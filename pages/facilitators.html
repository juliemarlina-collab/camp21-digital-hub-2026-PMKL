Facilitator Duty

Your Role at CAMP21

Facilitators are on-ground support, not evaluators. They rotate across groups, monitor work progress, guide checkpoints and support logistics.

Monitor Team Progress

Walk between team clusters, identify which phase each group is executing, and flag teams that appear stuck or off-track.

Guide Checkpoint Sign-offs

At each phase transition, confirm minimum template outputs before groups move to the next phase.

Support Logistics

Assist with material distribution, room movement, printing requests and urgent venue needs through the secretariat table.

Facilitator Checkpoint Checklist

Use these checks while monitoring assigned groups. These are support checks, not judging marks.

Before Moving Phase

Core
Confirm all required templates for the current phase are submitted or saved.
Check that students understand their problem, idea or prototype direction.
Ask guiding questions instead of giving direct solutions.
Update monitoring status for the assigned group.

Rotation Rule

Fairness
Do not stay with the same group for all phases.
Do not support your own institution's team if avoidable.
Escalate urgent academic or welfare concerns to the secretariat.
Keep judging-related information confidential.

Important Rules

Core conduct guidelines for all facilitators throughout the programme.

  1. You are on-ground support, not evaluators. Assessment is handled by the formal judging panel.
  2. Rotate across teams and avoid remaining with one group throughout the camp.
  3. Do not coach teams toward a specific solution; use prompting questions.
  4. Use the Smart DT App / companion dashboard to monitor phase milestone completions.
  5. Any participant welfare concern must be escalated immediately to the secretariat table.
