What is CAMP21?

Three Pillars of CAMP21

CAMP21 is built on three connected pillars: Design Thinking, AI Empowerment and SDG Impact. Each pillar is presented as a full-width poster showcase so participants can view the Design Thinking journey, AI empowerment and sustainability impact clearly.
